Cappuccino

WHAT YOU'LL NEED

1 Shot Espresso
3/5 Cup Milk of Your Choice
A Pinch Cinnamon or Chocolate Shavings (optional)

DIRECTIONS

Pour the espresso shot into your favourite mug.
Add the frothed milk or dairy alternative.
Sprinkle the cinnamon or chocolate shavings on top.

Americano

WHAT YOU'LL NEED

2 Shot Espresso
11/4 Cups Hot Water

DIRECTIONS

Boil the water and pour it into your favourite mug.
Slowly pour the espresso into the hot water.
